Cycle to Work Scheme!

The Cycle to Work Scheme opens on Monday 3rd November!

  • Save up to 42%* on the cost of a new bike as well as safety equipment if required
  • Spread the cost over 12 months
  • Save money on fuel and improve your fitness!
  • Choose your bike from halfords or an independent retailer

The cycle to work scheme gives Carillion employees the opportunity to aquire a new bike of their choice, and spread the cost over 12 months to make the benefit really affordable. Because the repayments will come straight from your gross salary, you'll save tax and National Insurance on the cost.

Halfords and independent retailers are included to give maximum choice, and you can spend up to £1,000 on a bike and safety accessories if needed.

Open from Monday 3rd November - 28th November. Don't miss out!

*based on a higher rate tax payer. This scheme is a salary sacrifice arrangement whereby employees have the opportunity to sacrifice part of their salary in exchange for a new cycle and safety equipment if required.
Employees will save tax and National Insurance on this amount and will have the amount deducted over a 12 month period.
